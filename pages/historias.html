The time a virus froze his google ads

They had a mishap, when a virus invaded one of theirs sites due to a security flaw, they had to debug the code to be able to delete it. It wasn't easy, they were able to remove it but Google blacklisted the website and kept us for 2 months without being able to post ads.

The time he missed 15 days of work

New people were being hired, the project was getting bigger and bigger. At one point there were so many branches that the works were stepped on before a demo and had to go with a local sample.

The time the power went out

He had to present a prototype of Figma, but the power went out and he couldn't do it, he had to explain the whole idea on a blackboard.

The time he merged and someone else merged

They were working on the same branch, we both knew and both assumed that no one had changes to merge at that time, the rest was a catastrophe.
